Blackened Shrimp Tacos Recipe

Here is the world's best shrimp tacos.

Click Here for Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Coleslaw mix
  • Chopped red onion
  • Large peeled shrimp
  • Cayenne seasoning
  • Flour tortillas

How to make the Tacos step-by-step

  1. Mix the coleslaw and chopped red onion together
  2. Season the raw shrimp in a seperate bowl
  3. Heat the skillet over medium-high heat. And cook the shrimp until they are bright pink on the outside. Approx 6 minutes
  4. Next heatup the tortillas over the same skillet
  5. Assemble the taco by placing the shrimp along with the coleslaw mix over top the tortilla
